The 19 Connection

The 9Teen is a popular Indian acoustic boy band originating from Visakhapatnam (Vizag), Andhra Pradesh. The band was formed in 2017 after the members met at a talent show where they were all 19 years old, which inspired their name.

Their Signature Sound

Known for high-energy live performances and soulful renditions, the trio has built a massive following by blending Hindi and English hits into unique acoustic mashups. They are famous for their "creative percussion," often using everyday items like spoons and knives to create rhythmic textures that have garnered millions of followers online.
